About Papa Louie's

Nestled in the heart of Andheri, Papa Louie’s Pizzeria is more than just a restaurant – it's a culinary sanctuary for lovers of authentic Italian flavors. With roots in traditional family recipes and a passion for excellence, we craft every pizza with the finest hand-picked ingredients, slow-cooked sauces, and love.
